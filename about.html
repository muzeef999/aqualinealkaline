About Us

At Medilight Hydrohealth Pvt Ltd, we are dedicated to transforming the way India experiences water. With a legacy rooted in innovation and excellence, we specialize in offering cutting-edge water purification solutions that prioritize health and wellness.

In collaboration with world-renowned experts like G&B Company of South Korea, we have introduced the revolutionary Healthy RO water purifiers to the Indian market. These advanced systems go beyond traditional purification, incorporating alkaline hydrogen water filters that are designed to enhance the quality of your drinking water, promoting better health and vitality.

Our commitment to quality is further strengthened through our partnership with ESLI Europe, a proud member of the Pollet Water Group. As the exclusive Indian partner for Domestic Aqualine water purifiers, Medilight is at the forefront of delivering superior water purification technologies that are trusted globally.

Since our inception in 2016 , Medilight has been a pioneer in Water Ionizer trading in India, backed by an extensive operational support team dedicated to marketing, sales, and research & development. Our mission is to bring the best of global water purification technology to every household in India, ensuring that our customers enjoy the purest, healthiest water possible.

Join us on our journey towards a healthier tomorrow, one drop at a time.
