Your Body is 70% Water​​

Did you know that 70% of your body is made up of water? Water is crucial for transporting oxygen, activating metabolism, and flushing out waste. Even a 1-2% drop in your body’s water levels can make you feel thirsty, and if not properly replenished, can lead to dehydration. This can slow down bodily functions, hinder waste elimination, and over time, contribute to aging and cellular damage.

How Medilight Aqualine Healthy Alkaline Hydrogen filter Works​​

The Medilight Aqualine Healthy Alkaline Hydrogen filter uses a unique 9-stage filtration process to transform ordinary water into hydrogen-rich alkaline water. Here's how it works:

  • Stage 1: Sediment Filter: Removes particulate matter, sediment, and other impurities from the water.
  • Stage 2: Activated Carbon Filter: Removes chlorine, taste, and odor from the water.
  • Stage 3: Reverse Osmosis (RO ) Filter: Removes dissolved solids, bacteria, viruses, and other impurities from the water.
  • Stage 4: Alkaline Filter: Adds alkaline minerals like calcium, magnesium, and potassium to the water.
  • Stage 5: Hydrogen Filter: Infuses molecular hydrogen (H2) into the water.
  • Stage 6: UV Filter: Kills bacteria, viruses, and other microorganisms in the water.
  • Stage 7: UF Filter: Removes remaining impurities and contaminants from the water.
  • Stage 8: TDS Control: Adjusts the water's total dissolved solids (TDS) to a healthy level.
  • Stage 9: Final Filter: Removes any remaining impurities and contaminants from the water.
